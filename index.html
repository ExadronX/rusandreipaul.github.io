Practical project about a traffic light intersection
Programs used: Proteus 8 Professional & Arduino IDE.

The project represents the traffic light of an intersection. I chose this intersection to consist of 2 streets and a pedestrian crossing. Each street will also be traffic lighted. In this project I used: 8 LEDs (3 LEDs for a car traffic light, another 3 LEDs for another car traffic light and 2 LEDs for a pedestrian traffic light), a button (to shorten the pedestrian waiting time), a speaker (for deaf people) and an LCD (LM017L, for counting the seconds).
